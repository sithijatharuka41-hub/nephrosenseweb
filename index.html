Research Foundation

Clinical Gaps & AI-Driven Solution

NephroSense bridges four key dimensions: predictive lifestyle analytics, automated ultrasound segmentation, urine dipstick quantification, and intelligent transplant matching.

Literature Synthesis

Existing works focus on isolated biomarkers; missing integration of lifestyle logs, imaging, and donor compatibility in real-time.

Research Gap

No unified AI framework combines YOLO urine analysis, U-Net kidney segmentation, lifestyle risk scoring, and donor ML ranking.

Novel Contribution

Four-component integration with explainable outputs (SHAP, risk scores) and real-time inference for resource-constrained settings.

AI Pipelines

Core Analytical Engines

Independently trained, jointly orchestrated: each component drives early detection and precision recommendations.

🏃‍♂️ Lifestyle & Clinical Risk Prediction

Random Forest classifier (89.5% accuracy) trained on demographics, BP, glucose, physical activity, diet logs. Risk levels: Low / Moderate / High. Rule-based engine generates personalized diet, hydration, sodium, exercise plans.

Random Forest XGBoost SHAP values
Actionable Outputs: Risk score (%), daily fluid intake, low-sodium meal suggestions, step goals. Validated with 94% clinical appropriateness.

🩻 U-Net Kidney Segmentation & Severity

U-Net CNN segments renal ultrasound; distance transform extracts cortex thickness. Severity: Healthy, Mild, Severe. Validation accuracy 89.88% (outperforms FCN, LinkNet).

Automated report: kidney region, max/min cortex thickness, severity stage, probability map. Real-time inference ready.

🧪 YOLOv8 + Dual-Channel CNN Urinalysis

YOLOv8n segments test pads from smartphone images; HSV features + illumination context via dual CNN. SVM classifier achieves 88.12% accuracy across protein, blood, glucose, pH. LAB white balancing robust to lighting.

Inference: 147ms per strip
8 biomarkers + overall risk stratification. Cost-effective CKD screening for remote areas.

🔗 ML Donor-Recipient Compatibility

Random Forest model processes blood type, HLA mismatch, age diff, BMI, dialysis duration, antibody levels → Compatibility Score (0-1). Accuracy 89.2%, precision/recall 0.89. SHAP feature transparency.

Ranked donor list: High (CS≥0.75), Moderate (0.5-0.75), Low (<0.5). Supports clinical transplant decisions.

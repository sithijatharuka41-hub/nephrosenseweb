System Capabilities

Four Integrated AI Modules

NephroSense removes diagnostic uncertainty so clinicians can focus on patient care, not manual interpretation.

Lifestyle Risk Prediction

Random Forest classifier with 89.5% accuracy predicts CKD progression. Generates personalized diet, hydration, and exercise plans tailored for Sri Lankan dietary patterns.

Ultrasound Segmentation

U-Net CNN segments kidney regions (89.88% validation accuracy). Extracts cortex thickness for Healthy/Mild/Severe classification without expensive CT/MRI.

Urine Strip Analysis

YOLOv8 + dual-channel CNN achieves 88.12% accuracy across 8 biomarkers. Robust to variable lighting — ideal for rural clinics.

Donor-Recipient Matching

Random Forest with SHAP explainability generates Compatibility Scores (0-1). Ranks donors: High (≥0.75), Moderate, Low.
